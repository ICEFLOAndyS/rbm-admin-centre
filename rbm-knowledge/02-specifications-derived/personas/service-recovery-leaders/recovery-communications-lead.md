# Recovery Communications Lead
## Responsibilities
Manages internal and external communications during service recovery, ensuring consistent and accurate messaging.

## Primary Interests
- Timely, accurate updates
- Alignment between operational reality and communications

## Challenges
- Conflicting information from recovery teams
- Rapidly changing recovery timelines

## Key RBM Features
- Real-time recovery status feeds
- Decision and milestone logging

## Before vs After
**Before:** Inconsistent messaging  
**After:** Aligned, evidence-based communications

## Value Proposition
RBM improves trust through accurate recovery communications.

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM provides a single source of truth for recovery status.
