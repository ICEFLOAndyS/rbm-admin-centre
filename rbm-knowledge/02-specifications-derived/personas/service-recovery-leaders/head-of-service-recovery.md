# Head of Service Recovery
## Responsibilities
Owns the organisation-wide Service Recovery capability. Defines recovery strategies, governance, and assurance for restoring Important Business Services following severe incidents.

## Primary Interests
- Predictable restoration of services
- Alignment to impact tolerances
- Executive and regulatory assurance

## Challenges
- Fragmented recovery ownership across teams
- Over-reliance on undocumented tribal knowledge
- Difficulty evidencing recovery capability

## Key RBM Features
- Executable service recovery runbooks
- Impact-tolerance aligned recovery orchestration
- Evidence capture for assurance

## Before vs After
**Before:** Reactive, ad-hoc recovery coordination  
**After:** Structured, governed service recovery execution

## Value Proposition
RBM operationalises service recovery, turning intent into proven capability.

## Regulatory Drivers
- FCA/PRA Operational Resilience
- DORA service continuity expectations

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM provides a single execution layer for service recovery.
