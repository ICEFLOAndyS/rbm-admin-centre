# Service Restoration Manager
## Responsibilities
Coordinates end-to-end restoration of services, ensuring dependencies are addressed in the correct sequence.

## Primary Interests
- Dependency-aware restoration
- Accurate progress tracking

## Challenges
- Hidden service dependencies
- Rework due to incorrect sequencing

## Key RBM Features
- Dependency modelling
- Sequenced task execution

## Before vs After
**Before:** Trial-and-error restoration  
**After:** Predictable, dependency-driven recovery

## Value Proposition
RBM improves restoration accuracy and speed.

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM ensures services are restored in the right order.
