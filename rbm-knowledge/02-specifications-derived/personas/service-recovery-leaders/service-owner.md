# Service Owner
## Responsibilities
Accountable for the health and recovery of specific business services.

## Primary Interests
- Minimal customer impact
- Clear recovery accountability

## Challenges
- Limited visibility during incidents
- Unclear ownership boundaries

## Key RBM Features
- Service-centric recovery views
- Ownership-aligned tasking

## Before vs After
**Before:** Limited influence over recovery  
**After:** Clear accountability and visibility

## Value Proposition
RBM gives Service Owners control and insight during recovery.
