# Commercial Manager
## Responsibilities
Manages commercial negotiations, pricing models, contract terms, and ongoing supplier performance.

## Primary Interests
- Predictable cost models
- Clear commercial terms
- Low-risk contractual commitments

## Challenges
- Ambiguous scope definitions
- Difficulty quantifying value of resilience tooling

## Key RBM Features
- Transparent subscription tiers
- Clearly defined service scope
- Measurable outcomes

## Before vs After
**Before:** Unclear commercials and scope creep  
**After:** Predictable, outcome-aligned commercial structures

## Value Proposition
RBM offers clear, scalable commercial models aligned to enterprise procurement norms.

## Procurement Role
**Enabler**

## Buying Triggers
- Multi-year SaaS procurement
- Framework-based sourcing

## Likely Objections
- “Pricing flexibility is unclear.”

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM provides predictable, scalable commercial models suitable for regulated enterprises.
