# Legal Counsel (Commercial / Technology)
## Responsibilities
Reviews contracts for legal, regulatory, and risk exposure. Ensures terms protect the organisation.

## Primary Interests
- Liability limitation
- Data protection and IP clarity
- Regulatory compliance

## Challenges
- Non-standard SaaS terms
- Ambiguous responsibilities during outages or failures

## Key RBM Features
- Clear demarcation of responsibilities
- Support for regulated-industry contractual terms

## Before vs After
**Before:** Lengthy legal negotiations  
**After:** Accelerated approval using familiar, low-risk terms

## Value Proposition
RBM aligns with enterprise legal expectations, reducing contract friction.

## Procurement Role
**Blocker (if concerns unresolved)**

## Buying Triggers
- Regulatory-driven contracts
- High-impact operational tooling

## Likely Objections
- “Liability exposure is unclear.”

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM is contract-ready for regulated enterprises with clear accountability boundaries.
