# Enterprise Risk Manager
## Responsibilities
Assesses and manages operational, technology, and third-party risk.

## Primary Interests
- Reduction of operational risk
- Evidence of control effectiveness
- Alignment to resilience frameworks

## Challenges
- Inability to quantify change and recovery risk
- Poor linkage between tooling and risk outcomes

## Key RBM Features
- Risk-relevant execution data
- Audit and control evidence

## Before vs After
**Before:** Qualitative risk assessments  
**After:** Data-driven risk insight

## Value Proposition
RBM turns execution data into meaningful risk reduction evidence.

## Procurement Role
**Strong Enabler / Qualifier**

## Buying Triggers
- DORA / OpRes remediation programmes
- Third-party risk reviews

## Likely Objections
- “How does this materially reduce risk?”

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM provides measurable risk reduction through controlled execution.
