# Vendor Management Office (VMO) Lead
## Responsibilities
Oversees supplier onboarding, performance management, and ongoing compliance.

## Primary Interests
- Supplier accountability
- Performance transparency
- Contractual compliance

## Challenges
- Limited operational visibility into supplier-delivered change
- Reactive vendor performance management

## Key RBM Features
- Execution transparency
- Evidence-based supplier performance insights

## Before vs After
**Before:** Retrospective vendor reviews  
**After:** Continuous, evidence-driven supplier oversight

## Value Proposition
RBM enables objective, operationally grounded vendor management.

## Procurement Role
**Qualifier**

## Buying Triggers
- Multi-supplier change programmes
- Regulatory focus on third-party risk

## Likely Objections
- “Supplier tooling should not be mandated.”

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM provides neutral, execution-level visibility across internal and third-party delivery.
