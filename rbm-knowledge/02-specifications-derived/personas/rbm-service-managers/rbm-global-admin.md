# RBM Global Admin
## Responsibilities
- Accountable for the health and availability of the Runbook Management (RBM) application.
- Responsible for the feedback loop with the vendor, ICEFLO Limited, including service issues and feature requests.
- Responsible for the scheduling and deployment of RBM releases.
- Responsible for the day-to-day administration of the RBM solution, including license allocation and user account management. 

## Primary Interests
- Effective setup and confiuration of the RBM solution
- Availability and performance of the RBM solution
- License and user management 

## Challenges
- Effective license management
- Defining, measuring and reporting on value derived from adoption of RBM
- Ease of user management procedures

## Key RBM Features
- Admin Centre
- License Management and Reporting

## Before vs After
**Before:** Spreadsheet runbooks offering limited value to the organisation, high risk profile and porr audit capability and compliance.
**After:** Measurable benefits across the enterprise, through positive use of RBM.

## Value Proposition
- RBM gives the Business users greater control, lower risk during go-liver cutovers
- RBM gives the Business users greater control and insight during service recoveries.
- RBM enhances the Operational Resiliece capabilities of the organsiation.
- RBM helps achieve complaince with internal and external auditor requirements.
- RBM reduces both the frequency and duration of service outages
- RBM as a service provided to Business colleagues enhances the value provided to the organisation by the RBM Service Owner.
