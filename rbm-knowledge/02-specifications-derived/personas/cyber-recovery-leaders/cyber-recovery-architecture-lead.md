# Cyber Recovery Architecture Lead
## Responsibilities
Designs and governs cyber recovery architectures, including isolated recovery environments, data vaults, and clean-room recovery patterns.

## Primary Interests
- Architectural integrity of recovery environments
- Separation from compromised production systems
- Repeatable recovery patterns

## Challenges
- Recovery architectures that exist only on paper
- Limited validation of end-to-end recovery designs

## Key RBM Features
- Architecture-aligned runbooks
- Dependency-aware recovery sequencing
- Validation through rehearsals

## Before vs After
**Before:** Theoretical recovery architectures  
**After:** Executable, validated recovery designs

## Value Proposition
RBM ensures cyber recovery architecture is operationally viable.

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM bridges cyber recovery design and execution.
