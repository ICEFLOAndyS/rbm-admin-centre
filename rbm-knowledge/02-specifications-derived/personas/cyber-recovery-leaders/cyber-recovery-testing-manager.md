# Cyber Recovery Testing Manager
## Responsibilities
Designs and executes cyber recovery exercises and simulations to validate recoverability under attack conditions.

## Primary Interests
- Realistic, meaningful cyber recovery testing
- Actionable insights and improvement

## Challenges
- Table-top exercises that do not test execution
- Poor capture of test outcomes and lessons learned

## Key RBM Features
- Scenario-driven cyber recovery testing
- Baseline vs actual comparison
- Evidence packs for assurance

## Before vs After
**Before:** Compliance-driven cyber tests  
**After:** Capability-driven recovery validation

## Value Proposition
RBM turns cyber recovery testing into continuous improvement.

## Regulatory Drivers
- DORA cyber resilience testing
- FCA/PRA expectations

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM makes cyber recovery testing real and defensible.
