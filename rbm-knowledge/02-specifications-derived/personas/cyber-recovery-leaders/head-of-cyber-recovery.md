# Head of Cyber Recovery

## Primary Interests
- Guaranteed recoverability after cyber incidents
- Clean, trusted recovery environments
- Separation of cyber recovery from standard DR

## Challenges
- Inability to trust compromised environments
- Poor coordination between cyber, infrastructure, and business teams
- Limited evidence that recovery will work under real attack conditions

## Key RBM Features
- Cyber-specific recovery runbooks
- Segregated recovery orchestration
- Evidence capture for recovery assurance

## Before vs After
**Before:** Ad-hoc cyber recovery plans with untested assumptions  
**After:** Orchestrated, proven cyber recovery execution

## Value Proposition
RBM provides confidence that cyber recovery is executable, repeatable, and defensible.

## Regulatory Drivers
- DORA ICT resilience and cyber recovery expectations
- FCA/PRA Operational Resilience

## Messaging & Positioning
