# Ransomware Recovery Manager
## Responsibilities
Plans and executes recovery from ransomware events, including data restoration, system rebuild, and service reintroduction.

## Primary Interests
- Clean recovery without reinfection
- Measured, staged service restoration
- Assurance over data integrity

## Challenges
- Pressure to restore quickly without validation
- Complex sequencing across systems and teams

## Key RBM Features
- Ransomware-specific recovery workflows
- Validation checkpoints
- Controlled reintroduction of services

## Before vs After
**Before:** Risky, rushed recoveries  
**After:** Controlled, evidence-driven restoration

## Value Proposition
RBM reduces the risk of reinfection and recovery failure.

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM enables safe, structured ransomware recovery.
