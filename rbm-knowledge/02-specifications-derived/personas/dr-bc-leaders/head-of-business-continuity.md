# Head of Business Continuity
## Responsibilities
Owns the organisation-wide Business Continuity Management System (BCMS). Ensures continuity strategies, plans, and exercises protect Important Business Services and meet regulatory expectations.

## Primary Interests
- Proven continuity capability
- Alignment to impact tolerances
- Evidence of end-to-end resilience

## Challenges
- Static BC plans that are rarely exercised
- Limited integration between BC plans and live operational execution
- Difficulty evidencing effectiveness to regulators

## Key RBM Features
- Executable continuity runbooks
- Scenario-based rehearsal evidence
- Dependency-aware service recovery orchestration

## Before vs After
**Before:** Document-heavy BC plans with limited execution confidence  
**After:** Executable, tested continuity capability with evidence

## Value Proposition
RBM operationalises Business Continuity, turning plans into proven, repeatable execution.

## Regulatory Drivers
- FCA/PRA Operational Resilience
- ISO 22301 principles

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM bridges the gap between continuity planning and real-world execution.
