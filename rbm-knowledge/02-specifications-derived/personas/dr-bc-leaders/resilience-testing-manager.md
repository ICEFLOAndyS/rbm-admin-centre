# Resilience Testing Manager
## Responsibilities
Designs and executes resilience and continuity testing programmes across business services, technology, and suppliers.

## Primary Interests
- Meaningful testing outcomes
- Continuous improvement
- Evidence-based assurance

## Challenges
- Tests that lack realism
- Poor linkage between tests and real incidents
- Inconsistent evidence capture

## Key RBM Features
- Scenario-driven testing orchestration
- Baseline vs actual comparison
- Evidence packs for audits

## Before vs After
**Before:** Tick-box testing exercises  
**After:** Insight-driven resilience validation

## Value Proposition
RBM turns resilience testing into a measurable improvement cycle.

## Regulatory Drivers
- DORA resilience testing
- FCA/PRA Operational Resilience

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM elevates testing from compliance to capability.
