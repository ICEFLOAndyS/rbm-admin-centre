# Disaster Recovery Lead
## Responsibilities
Designs, maintains, and executes Disaster Recovery strategies and exercises. Ensures systems and services can be recovered within agreed tolerances.

## Primary Interests
- Proven recoverability
- Repeatable, low-risk DR execution
- Accurate recovery metrics

## Challenges
- DR tests treated as one-off events
- Manual coordination across technology teams
- Poor capture of actual recovery data

## Key RBM Features
- DR-specific execution runbooks
- Real-time tracking of recovery tasks
- Capture of actual RTO/RPO performance

## Before vs After
**Before:** Manual DR tests with anecdotal outcomes  
**After:** Measured, auditable recovery execution

## Value Proposition
RBM provides objective proof of recoverability and accelerates DR confidence.

## Regulatory Drivers
- DORA ICT resilience testing
- FCA/PRA Operational Resilience

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM makes DR testing repeatable, measurable, and defensible.
