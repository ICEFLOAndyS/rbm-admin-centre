# Service Recovery Manager
## Responsibilities
Leads coordinated recovery of business services during major incidents. Orchestrates people, process, and technology recovery activities.

## Primary Interests
- Clear command and control
- Fast, coordinated service restoration
- Minimal customer impact

## Challenges
- Fragmented recovery actions
- Poor visibility of recovery progress
- High cognitive load during crises

## Key RBM Features
- Live recovery dashboards
- Dependency-driven task sequencing
- Integrated communications

## Before vs After
**Before:** Reactive, stressful recovery coordination  
**After:** Structured, controlled service recovery

## Value Proposition
RBM reduces chaos during incidents and improves recovery outcomes.

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM provides a single control plane for service recovery execution.
