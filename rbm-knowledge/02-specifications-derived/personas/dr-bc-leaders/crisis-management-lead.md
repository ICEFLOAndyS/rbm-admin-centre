# Crisis Management Lead
## Responsibilities
Coordinates executive-level response to severe operational crises. Ensures timely decision-making, communication, and escalation.

## Primary Interests
- Situational awareness
- Decision traceability
- Clear escalation paths

## Challenges
- Information overload during crises
- Lack of auditable decision records
- Disconnected operational updates

## Key RBM Features
- Executive-level situational dashboards
- Decision and action logging
- Integration with recovery runbooks

## Before vs After
**Before:** Fragmented updates and undocumented decisions  
**After:** Controlled crisis response with full traceability

## Value Proposition
RBM supports confident, defensible crisis leadership.

## Messaging & Positioning
RBM connects crisis governance with operational execution.
