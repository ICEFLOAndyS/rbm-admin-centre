## 1. Vision statement
- RBM provides a governed, auditable, and automated approach to managing runbooks for regulated operational scenarios.
- RBM provides a common solution for 4 Operational Resilience challenges, namely:
  - Planned Change
  - Service Recovery
  - Disaster Recovery / Business Continuity
  - Cyber ecovery

## 2. Strategic objectives
- Replace spreadsheet-based runbooks
- RBM is viewed as the go-to solution for all ServiceNow customers
- Support operational resilience obligations (e.g. FCA/PRA, DORA)
- Enable controlled automation and human-in-the-loop execution

## 3. Success measures
- Increased confidence in operational resilience posture
- Reduction in execution errors
- Improved audit outcomes
- Faster, safer delivery of planned change
- Faster service recovery
- Faster cyber recovery
- Fasster disaster recovery
